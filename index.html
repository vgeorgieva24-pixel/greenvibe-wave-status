How it works

From money to verified impact — in one loop

No anonymous donations disappearing into reports. Every contribution becomes a mission on the map, and every mission ends with photo, GPS and community-verified proof.

💚

1 · Fund

A Patron funds a cleanup in a zone they care about. The payment converts into Eco Coins and spawns a live mission — visible to everyone on the map.

🧤

2 · Clean

A Doer claims the mission, does the cleanup, and submits before & after photos with GPS evidence straight from the field.

✅

3 · Verify & reward

Evidence is validated. The Doer earns Karma Coins and levels up; the Patron sees exactly what their money achieved — in kilograms, square meters and CO₂.

Three ways to join

A role for everyone who cares

Whether you give your money, your time or your voice — your contribution is visible, verified and rewarded.

💚

Patrons

Fund cleanups in the places you love. Watch your impact grow on your personal dashboard — missions, kilograms, square meters, CO₂ — and download your verified impact report.

🧤

Doers

Turn your energy into visible change. Claim missions near you, earn Karma Coins and XP, level up from First Steps to Wave Maker, and build your reputation as a verified eco-hero.

📣

Ambassadors

Grow the movement. Bring in new Patrons and Doers, track your network's real-world impact, and earn recognition — and rewards — for every verified conversion.
